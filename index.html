Who We Are

Odisha's Trusted Platform for Financial Education

"An informed investor is a protected investor. Our mission is to ensure no one in Odisha makes a financial decision out of ignorance or fear."

Founded in 2015, the Odisha Investors Society is a registered non-profit organization committed to improving financial literacy among retail investors, working professionals, and first-time investors across Odisha. We are not affiliated with any brokerage, fund house, or financial product distributor.

We conduct workshops, seminars, and awareness programs covering personal finance, equity investing, mutual funds, taxation, and retirement planning — all at no cost or nominal cost to participants. Our only goal is education.

Our Mission

To democratize financial education across Odisha by conducting accessible, unbiased, and practical programs that empower every citizen — regardless of income, background, or education — to make sound financial decisions and protect their wealth.

Our Vision

A financially literate Odisha where every investor understands their rights, recognizes investment risks, and participates confidently in India's growth story — free from fraud, misinformation, and exploitative financial advice.
